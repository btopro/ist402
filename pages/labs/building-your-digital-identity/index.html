Lab 1 / first class

This is a warm up lab that will help set the stage for the platforms we'll use for the rest of the semester.

  1. Create a Slack account and join two slack channels
    • IST 402 - Slack Invite
      • We'll use this as the primary working space / link sharing / communication space for the course
    • ELMS: Learning Network - Slack Invite
      • Me and my band of nuts are available to you. If you want to get more involved in this space beyond what we'll experience in class or have questions about the education or web industry, the door is always open
      • There's developers and educational professionals from multiple universities there, IST and otherwise.
  2. Think up a brand / identity / persona for your online identity as an IT professional
    • What does your brand feel like?
      • Great Explainer? Hero's Journey? Future CIO?
    • Pick a name that you can attempt to use across "all platforms"
      • Domain name, Github, Twitter, LinkedIn, YouTube
    • Note: You MAY want to divorce this from your real persona / personal accounts. Everyone does it differently in industry; many merge, there are risks associated with that. I fragment on YouTube  but generally keep all mine unified.
    • Because we haven't started a blog yet (but let's get in the swing of doing a write up and a video), write up a paragraph describing your brand in Word/Docs
  3. Register this account on Github, Twitter, LinkedIn, YouTube
    • Be sure to set the username to be the same across them
  4. Create a Playlist on YouTube called "Clarissa explains it all", replacing Clarissa with your persona
  5. Create a screencast that's 3 - 5 minutes long that includes the following:
    • Pick a screencasting solution from the Screencasting resources area
    • introducing your channel from a technology professional context
    • Showing your favorite tech blog / anything you've read and enjoy learning from online currently. Talk about an article on the blog while it's being shown in order to practice the explainer concept.
    • End with what piece of technology you'd like to learn more about how it works from the topics in the survey
  6. Post a link to this video on your social accounts (Twitter / LinkedIn) with some setup to try and draw people to it using 3 hashtags.
  7. Use the IST402 Slack directly  and upload your Word/Doc file to the channel named #lab-1-brand-management.   Make sure to include a link to your YouTube Channel and Video post in the bottom of your "blog" post
  8. Extra if you'd like
    1. If you @btopro on twitter I'll RT to increase reach
    2. @EdTechJoker is an account that will post into the "Socialmedia" channel in our slack channel. You're welcome to use this for social reach / to try it out as well
